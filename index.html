Biography

I’m a PhD student at Imperial College London, working with Dr Ali Mashayek. My research involves using mathematical and numerical models to understand oceanic fluid dynamics. In particular, I’m interested in internal waves and vortices that are generated when deep oceanic flows interact with submarine topography.

I’m a part of the Centre for Doctoral Training in Mathematics of Planet Earth and the Grantham Institute’s Science and Solutions for a Changing Planet Doctoral Training Partnership.
