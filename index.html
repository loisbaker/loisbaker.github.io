Biography

I’m a postdoctoral fellow in the School of Mathematics at the University of Edinburgh, funded by a Flora Philip Fellowship and the EPSRC National Fellowships in Fluid Dynamics. My research involves using mathematical and numerical models to understand oceanic fluid dynamics. In particular, I’m interested in the interactions of internal waves and submesoscale vortices that are generated in the deep and upper ocean.

Interests
